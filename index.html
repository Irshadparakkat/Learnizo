One-on-One Tuition

Learnizo is an online tuition platform where we provide live interactive 1-on-1 tuition. We ensure that you get the best-personalised classes based on your needs. That means, in Learnizo, you can opt for all chapters or specific chapters of a subject where you need special attention. While you are at Learnizo, you pay only for what you learn. Group Tuition

Learnizo provides group tuition for various courses, including Grade 1-12, BTech, MTech, BCA, MCA, Bsc and Other UG and PG courses. We also conduct the batch tuition for various competitive exams like Kerala PSC. We schedule these tuition classes, and you can attend the sessions in our virtual classroom. You either choose complete course tuition, or you can join the sessions related to specific subjects/modules/chapters. You pay only for what you opted.
